About Us                         

 Penny's Produce was first started when Joseph Penny bought the property where we currently are in 1951. Mr. Penny always treated everyone he came across as if they were family, so you can be sure that if you stop by you'll be treated like family. We have proudly been apart of the Garner community for five decades and plan on being here for plenty more. Over the years we have watched as the town of Garner grows and we have grow right along side her. Each year we try to bring something new and exciting for everyone to enjoy.
